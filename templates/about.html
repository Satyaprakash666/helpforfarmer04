Our Story

Founded in 2025, Green Acres Farm began with a simple mission: to grow organic produce while preserving the land for future generations. What started as a small family operation has grown into a community hub where sustainable farming meets education and connection.

Our Values

Sustainability

We implement regenerative farming practices that improve soil health, conserve water, and reduce our carbon footprint.

Organic Farming

We grow without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring our produce is as natural and nutritious as possible.

Community Involvement

We believe in the power of community. Through farm tours, workshops, and volunteer programs, we connect people with the land.

"The best fertilizer is the farmer's shadow. We work with nature, not against it, to create abundance for our community."
- Satya Prakash Shrivastava, Founder
